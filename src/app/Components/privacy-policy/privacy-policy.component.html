Introduction

The Local Basket values your privacy. We’re committed to protecting your personal information in compliance with Canada’s PIPEDA and relevant provincial laws. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website, place an order, or subscribe to our services. We are dedicated to ensuring that your personal data is handled responsibly, transparently, and securely. By understanding our practices, you can make informed decisions about the information you share with us, and feel confident that your privacy is our top priority.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website, place an order, or subscribe to our services.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to serve you better. This may include:

  • Contact details: name, email, phone number, delivery address
  • Payment information: processed securely through third-party providers (we do not store payment details)
  • Order details: products purchased, preferences, transaction history
  • Website usage: cookies, browser type, and anonymous analytics data
  • Marketing preferences: your consent to receive updates or promotions

How We Use Your Information

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Communicate with you about purchases or inquiries
  • Send newsletters, promotions, and seasonal updates (with your consent)
  • Improve our products, services, and website experience
  • Meet legal and accounting obligations

We will never sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

Consent

By submitting your information (for example, placing an order or subscribing to our newsletter), you consent to our collection and use of your data as outlined here.

You can withdraw your consent at any time by emailing privacy@thelocalbasket.ca.

Sharing Your Information

We only share limited personal information with trusted service providers who help us operate our business, including:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe, Shopify Payments)
  • Shipping and courier services
  • Email and marketing tools (e.g., Mailchimp)
  • Web hosting and analytics platforms

These partners maintain strict confidentiality and use your data only for the services they provide. We may also disclose your information when required by law.

Security and Data Retention

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data through encryption, password protection, and secure servers.

Your information is kept only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes listed in this policy or as required by law. Afterwards, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses cookies to improve functionality and analyze site traffic. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work properly without them.

We may use Google Analytics or similar tools to collect anonymous usage data to help enhance your browsing experience.

Your Rights

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections or updates
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Request deletion of your personal data, where legally possible

To exercise these rights, please contact us directly.
